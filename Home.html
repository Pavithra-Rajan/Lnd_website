About Us

The Literary & Debating Club of NIT Calicut (National Institute of Technology Calicut) is one of the oldest and the most prestigious clubs of NIT-C. Ever since its birth in the REC times, the club has taken up mantle of providing the students a rostrum to flaunt their belletristic, oratory and expressive genius.Kindled by the passion for the world of literature, the club is a stage to express, enunciate and extemporize. Through the years, we have been striving persistently to spread our expense in close accordance with our rule of thumb, 'Deeds not Words'. Organizing competitions in extempore, elocution, debates, quizzes and so much has helped the club live up to and beyond the expectations for which it was set up.

School Debutante

The Literary and Debating Club's School Debutante is a treasure trove of interesting events. It is a social initiative to help students enrich their literary and orator skills. It contains a plethora of events which assess students' intellectual sharpness and exactitude, develop assertiveness and at the same time enhance their knowledge.

Conclave

An open platform for the intellectual and conventional minds to introspect on world-pressing issues and its out-turns. Together we explore the various angles and broaden the mindset of the speakers as well as the audience. This open discussion may or may not end in a consensus but there's always something to take away in the end.

Page Turners

Book reading is a tradition that is increasingly becoming a thing of the past. In our fast-paced technology fuelled lives, where is the time to kick off our boots and settle down smugly with a nice book? But book lovers will know the joy of reading a good book, the thrill with which each page is flipped, the tears and laughter captured within those pages, and the profound knowledge they can impart. It's time we rekindled the book culture by sharing the books we possess with each other.

As the clock ticks away, we run the race with busy lives and toppling schedules. Little do we care to smile at the faces that pass by? There are souls that brim with countless moments of pain and sacrifice. With HONIT: Humans of NIT, we bring to your hearts the tales that capture the beat of humanity, tales that capture the rhythm of life.

Book Club

We all have moments when we wish for someone to hear all our feelings about Octavia Butler over a cup of coffee. Whenever we read a good book, we spend every waking moment wanting to talk about that book. That’s where a “Book Club” comes in, especially now when we have to look for things to do to break out of the monotony of the lockdown. Be a part of a crowd where you can share your heart out about the books you find repose in and listen from others how it made them feel.
